✦ WHAT IS FREEZY-OS

Freezy-OS is a browser-based operating system developed by FRZ Generations. It functions like an advanced virtual desktop environment accessible via any modern web browser, and optionally as a desktop application (.exe) or mobile app (.apk).

Freezy-OS includes built-in tools for productivity, coding, study, communication, religious use, and more — all free of charge.

✦ FEATURES INCLUDED

  • Code Editor with 60+ language support (Judge0 powered)
  • Office Suite — Word, Excel, Whiteboard
  • PDF Tools — Split, Merge
  • Study Tools — Quiz, Flashcard, Question Paper, Timer
  • Maps with directions (OpenStreetMap)
  • Weather from your location
  • Prayer Times / Azan Times (Aladhan API)
  • Hijri Calendar, Malayalam Islamic Calendar, Religious Calendars
  • Calculator and Unit Converter
  • QR Code Generator, URL Shortener, Image Host, Media Host
  • WhatsApp Message Converter
  • Music Player
  • File Manager (Google Drive backed)
  • QR Connect — sync phone session to PC
  • Custom Search Engine
  • Public Files Library
  • Community Tools Section
  • Fully customizable desktop — themes, wallpapers, icons

✦ WHAT DATA WE COLLECT (HONESTLY)

We believe in full transparency. Here is exactly what Freezy-OS collects and why:

  • Google account name, email, profile picture — collected via Firebase Authentication when you sign in. Required to identify your account.
  • Files you create and save — stored in YOUR OWN Google Drive under a folder named Software/[YourUsername]/. We do not read or access any other files in your Drive.
  • OS preferences — theme, wallpaper, settings are stored in Firebase Firestore under your user ID. This keeps your OS personalized across devices.
  • Location data — accessed only when you use Maps, Weather, or Prayer Times features. Your location is NOT stored permanently on our servers.
  • QR Connect session tokens — temporary tokens used to sync your phone and PC session. These are automatically deleted after your session ends.
  • IP address — logged ONLY if you attempt to access Developer Tools more than once. This is for platform security. See Security Policy below.
  • Login activity — timestamp, login method, and device type are logged for account security purposes.

✦ WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

  • We do NOT sell your data to any third party.
  • We do NOT read the contents of your Google Drive files beyond the Software/[Username]/ folder we create.
  • We do NOT store your location permanently.
  • We do NOT track your browsing activity inside the iframed tools.
  • We do NOT collect payment information (Freezy-OS is completely free).
  • We do NOT share your personal data with advertisers.
